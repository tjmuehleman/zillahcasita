Wineries

Our favorite wineries near the house — from bold Zillah reds to laid-back tasting rooms worth the drive.

Sheridan Vineyards

3 miles from the house

Sheridan Vineyard & Winery is a Zillah wine staple known for bold reds, zero pretense, and consistently excellent bottles. It’s the kind of tasting room where the wine speaks for itself and you’re encouraged to slow down, enjoy the pour, and stop overthinking the experience.

Dichotomy (Outlook)

14.5 miles from the house

Dichotomy Winery is a relaxed, standout Yakima Valley winery run by Australian couple Rosie and Jared. With thoughtfully made wines and a new outdoor tasting area that’s perfect for lingering, it’s the kind of place that invites you to slow down, pour another glass, and stay awhile.

Read more →

Two Mountain Winery (Zillah)

2.5 miles from the house

Two Mountain Winery is the kind of Yakima Valley winery that takes great wine seriously without taking itself too seriously. With a new tasting room, a kid-friendly clubhouse, and the always-intriguing Bros tasting, it’s a spot that works just as well for families as it does for wine lovers.
